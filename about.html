Valeria is a visual artist, UX/UI designer and web developer based in Brooklyn, NY.



Who I am ↓

I have always been two things: an engineer and an artist. I use both sides of my brain to create beautiful solutions to everyday problems in design and code. I love understanding the way things work, how everything is built - a camera, a building and even a chocolate cake.
My eye understands how color and empathy work together to provoke a feeling. Although I work in media, my biggest passions go beyond a digital screen. I love baking cookies, creating structures and taking disposable photographs.
